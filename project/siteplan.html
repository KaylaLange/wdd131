Final Project Site Plan

Beyond the Bite: Adventures in Dining

This name represents a site that is focused on bringing information on unique dining experiences around the world to the user.

For the Adventurous Eater

This is an informational site that shares info about unique culinary experiences around the world and encourages the user to sign-up for the Beyond the Bite monthly newsletter to better help them plan their next adventure.

For the Adventurous Eater

  • Where should my next culinary travel destination be?
  • What are people saying about these experiences who've already been?
  • How can I stay in the loop with new unique dining experiences?
